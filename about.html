// about

Built because
it was needed.

Vitaq wasn't designed by a fitness brand or funded by venture capital. It started as a personal tool — built by someone who needed to stay healthy and active, and couldn't find an app that did the job without the baggage.

// the why

Sitting at a desk all day has consequences.

Between a demanding career and two young kids, it's easy to let fitness slide. The work is there, the meetings are there, the exhaustion is there. And before long, you realize you're not as sharp, not as energetic, not as present as you want to be.

That realization was the starting point for Vitaq. Not a desire to build a fitness company — just a genuine need to track workouts, monitor nutrition, and stay consistent without making it complicated. The kind of app that gets out of your way and lets you focus on showing up.

When nothing on the market felt right — too cluttered, too gamified, too willing to trade your health data for ad revenue — the decision was simple: build it.

// what we believe
🔒

Your data stays yours

Everything you log — workouts, nutrition, body stats — lives on your device. No servers. No accounts. No third parties with access to your health information.

⌚

Your watch should work with you

Apple Watch is the best fitness companion ever made. Vitaq treats it that way — real-time bidirectional sync so you can log sets from your wrist without reaching for your phone.

🎯

Consistency over perfection

The goal isn't to optimize every macro or hit a PR every session. It's to show up regularly, track honestly, and build habits that last — especially when life is full.

Vitaq is for the parent who squeezes in a workout before the kids wake up. The professional who eats lunch at their desk and still wants to know what they're putting in their body. The person who doesn't need a leaderboard or a streak notification — just a clear picture of how they're doing.

It was built for personal use and shared because the problem isn't unique. If any of this sounds familiar, Vitaq was made for you too.
